I am M. Phil Scholar at Quaid-i-Azam University, Islamabad with a Assistant Professor Dr. Akmal Saeed Khattak. I have completed my bachelors (2021) from University of Buner under the supervision of Dr. Inayat Ullah


My final year research focuses on Point-of-Interest Recommendation using Graph Neural Network. The purpose of the research is to bridge the gap of personalized recommendation incorporating spatial, temporal, social, and textual features using graph neural network. My objective is to solve complex social problems by using the power of graph neural network to ease the way of living, and facilitate people with relevant information.


Despite my research background, I have hands-on experience machine learning, deep learning, graph neural networks, and data science. Furthermore, I have skills related to web technologies, databases, designing workflows & architectures, and project management skills which has been taught by Google on Coursera.
